The 3 Compounding S-Curves Framework

Core Thesis: Crypto's growth follows three sequential, compounding s-curves. Each phase builds on the previous, creating exponential growth opportunities. We're currently transitioning from Phase 2 (Accumulation) to Phase 3 (Utilization).

🪙 Phase 1: Asset Creation 2009-2024
Tokenization of Value

The creation phase started with Bitcoin in 2009 and expanded to include layer-1 monetary assets, project tokens, stablecoins, content coins, memecoins, NFTs, and tokenized equities. From 2024-2025, the industry moved up the steepest part of the s-curve.

Key Milestone: Growth from ~20,000 tradable tokens to millions in just a few years.

🏛️ Regulatory Milestone: GENIUS Act

The Guiding and Establishing National Innovation for US Stablecoins Act (GENIUS Act)

  • Federal regulatory framework for stablecoin issuers
  • Reserve requirements: 100% backing with high-quality liquid assets
  • Regular audits and transparency requirements
  • Enables institutional and enterprise adoption at scale
  • Positions US as global leader in stablecoin innovation
